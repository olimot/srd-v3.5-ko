주문 목록 I

An Mor Fappearing at the end of a spell’s name in the spell lists denotes a spell with a material or focus component, respectively, that is not normally included in a spell component pouch. An Xdenotes a spell with an XP component paid by the caster.

제시 순서: In the spell lists and the spell descriptions that follow them, the spells are presented in alphabetical order by name except for those belonging to certain spell chains.

When a spell’s name begins with “lesser,” “greater,” or “mass,” the spell description is alphabetized under the second word of the spell name instead.

히트 다이스: The term “Hit Dice” is used synonymously with “character levels” for effects that affect a number of Hit Dice of creatures. Creatures with Hit Dice only from their race, not from classes, have character levels equal to their Hit Dice.

시전자 레벨: A spell’s power often depends on caster level, which is defined as the caster’s class level for the purpose of casting a particular spell. A creature with no classes has a caster level equal to its Hit Dice unless otherwise specified. The word “level” in the spell lists that follow always refers to caster level.

생명체 및 캐릭터: The words “creature” and “character” are used synonymously in the spell descriptions.

  6. 6 치료: Cures 10 points/level of damage, all diseases and mental conditions.
  7. 7 재생: Subject’s severed limbs grow back, cures 4d8 damage +1/level (max +35).
  8. 8 치명적인 상처 치유, 광역: Cures 4d8 damage +1/level (max +40) for many creatures.
  9. 9 치료, 광역: As heal, but with several subjects.

지식 계열

보장된 능력: Add all Knowledge skills to your list of cleric class skills.

You cast divination spells at +1 caster level.

지식 계열 주문
  1. 1 비밀문 탐지: Reveals hidden doors within 60 ft.
  2. 2 생각 탐지: Allows “listening” to surface thoughts.
  3. 3 투청/투시: Hear or see at a distance for 1 min./level.
  4. 4 점술M: Provides useful advice for specific proposed actions.
  5. 5 진실의 시야M: Lets you see all things as they really are.
  6. 6 길 찾기: Shows most direct way to a location.
  7. 7 전설의 지식MF: Lets you learn tales about a person, place, or thing.
  8. 8 장소 식별: Reveals exact location of creature or object.
  9. 9 예언: “Sixth sense” warns of impending danger.

질서 계열

보장된 능력: You cast law spells at +1 caster level.

질서 계열 주문
  1. 1 혼돈 방어 마법: +2 to AC and saves, counter mind control, hedge out elementals and outsiders.
  2. 2 평온한 감정: Calms creatures, negating emotion effects.
  3. 3 혼돈 방어 마법진: As protection spells, but 10-ft. radius and 10 min./level.
  4. 4 질서의 격노: Damages and dazes chaotic creatures.
  5. 5 혼돈 무효화: +4 bonus against attacks by chaotic creatures.
  6. 6 몬스터 포박: As hold person, but any creature.
  7. 7 어르신의 말씀: Kills, paralyzes, slows, or deafens nonlawful subjects.
  8. 8 질서의 방패F: +4 to AC, +4 resistance, and SR 25 against chaotic spells.
  9. 9 9레벨 몬스터 소환*: Calls extraplanar creature to fight for you.

*Cast as a law spell only.

행운 계열

보장된 능력: You gain the power of good fortune, which is usable once per day. This extraordinary ability allows you to reroll one roll that you have just made before the DM declares whether the roll results in success or failure. You must take the result of the reroll, even if it’s worse than the original roll.

행운 계열 주문
  1. 1 혼돈의 방패: Ranged attacks against you have 20% miss chance.
  2. 2 보조: +1 on attack rolls, +1 against fear, 1d8 temporary hp +1/level (max +10).
  3. 3 에너지 방어 마법: Absorb 12 points/level of damage from one kind of energy.
  4. 4 이동의 자유: Subject moves normally despite impediments.
  5. 5 부여술 파괴: Frees subjects from enchantments, alterations, curses, and petrification.
  6. 6 오도: Turns you invisible and creates illusory double.
  7. 7 주문 반사: Reflect 1d4+6 spell levels back at caster.
  8. 8 순간 선견: You gain insight bonus on single attack roll, check, or save.
  9. 9 기적X: Requests a deity’s intercession.

마법 계열

보장된 능력: Use scrolls, wands, and other devices with spell completion or spell trigger activation as a wizard of one-half your cleric level (at least 1st level). For the purpose of using a scroll or other magic device, if you are also a wizard, actual wizard levels and these effective wizard levels stack.

마법 계열 주문
  1. 1 마법 오라: Alters object’s magic aura.
  2. 2 식별: Determines properties of magic item.
  3. 3 마법 무효화: Cancels magical spells and effects.
  4. 4 주문의 능력을 부여: Transfer spells to subject.
  5. 5 주문 저항: Subject gains SR 12 + level.
  6. 6 대마법 보호장: Negates magic within 10 ft.
  7. 7 주문 반사: Reflect 1d4+6 spell levels back at caster.
  8. 8 주문 방어 마법MF: Confers +8 resistance bonus.
  9. 9 마법사의 분열: Dispels magic, disenchants magic items.

식물 계열

보장된 능력: Rebuke or command plant creatures as an evil cleric rebukes or commands undead. Use this ability a total number of times per day equal to 3 + your Charisma modifier. This granted power is a supernatural ability.

Add Knowledge (nature) to your list of cleric class skills.

식물 계열 주문
  1. 1 휘감기: Plants entangle everyone in 40-ft.-radius.
  2. 2 나무껍질피부: Grants +2 (or higher) enhancement to natural armor.
  3. 3 식물 성장: Grows vegetation, improves crops.
  4. 4 식물 명령: Sway the actions of one or more plant creatures.
  5. 5 가시 장벽: Thorns damage anyone who tries to pass.
  6. 6 나무 퇴치: Pushes away wooden objects.
  7. 7 살아 움직이는 식물: One or more trees animate and fight for you.
  8. 8 식물 조종: Control actions of one or more plant creatures.
  9. 9 휘청거리는 자: Summons 1d4+2 shambling mounds to fight for you.

보호 계열

보장된 능력: You can generate a protective ward as a supernatural ability. Grant someone you touch a resistance bonus equal to your cleric level on his or her next saving throw. Activating this power is a standard action. The protective ward is an abjuration effect with a duration of 1 hour that is usable once per day.

보호 계열 주문
  1. 1 성역: Opponents can’t attack you, and you can’t attack.
  2. 2 타인의 방패F: You take half of subject’s damage.
  3. 3 에너지 방어 마법: Absorb 12 points/level of damage from one kind of energy.
  4. 4 주문 면역: Subject is immune to one spell per four levels.
  5. 5 주문 저항: Subject gains SR 12 + level.
  6. 6 대마법 보호장: Negates magic within 10 ft.
  7. 7 격퇴: Creatures can’t approach you.
  8. 8 정신 공백: Subject is immune to mental/emotional magic and scrying.
  9. 9 무지갯빛의 구: As prismatic wall, but surrounds on all sides.

근력 계열

보장된 능력: You can perform a feat of strength as a supernatural ability. You gain an enhancement bonus to Strength equal to your cleric level. Activating the power is a free action, the power lasts 1 round, and it is usable once per day.

근력 계열 주문
  1. 1 인간형 거대화: Humanoid creature doubles in size.
  2. 2 황소의 근력: Subject gains +4 to Str for 1 min./level.
  3. 3 마법의 예복: Armor or shield gains +1 enhancement per four levels.
  4. 4 주문 면역: Subject is immune to one spell per four levels.
  5. 5 의로운 권능: Your size increases, and you gain combat bonuses.
  6. 6 돌피부M: Ignore 10 points of damage per attack.
  7. 7 붙잡는 손: Large hand provides cover, pushes, or grapples.
  8. 8 꽉 쥔 주먹: Large hand provides cover, pushes, or attacks your foes.
  9. 9 짓누르는 손: Large hand provides cover, pushes, or crushes your foes.

태양 계열

보장된 능력: Once per day, you can perform a greater turning against undead in place of a regular turning. The greater turning is like a normal turning except that the undead creatures that would be turned are destroyed instead.

태양 계열 주문
  1. 1 원소 견디기: Exist comfortably in hot or cold environments.
  2. 2 금속 가열: Make metal so hot it damages those who touch it.
  3. 3 타오르는 빛: Ray deals 1d8/two levels, more against undead.
  4. 4 화염 방패: Creatures attacking you take fire damage; you’re protected from heat or cold.
  5. 5 화염 타격: Smite foes with divine fire (1d6/level damage).
  6. 6 불꽃 씨앗: Acorns and berries become grenades and bombs.
  7. 7 태양광선: Beam blinds and deals 4d6 damage.
  8. 8 태양폭발: Blinds all within 10 ft., deals 6d6 damage.
  9. 9 무지갯빛의 구: As prismatic wall, but surrounds on all sides.

여행 계열

보장된 능력: For a total time per day of 1 round per cleric level you possess, you can act normally regardless of magical effects that impede movement as if you were affected by the spell freedom of movement. This effect occurs automatically as soon as it applies, lasts until it runs out or is no longer needed, and can operate multiple times per day (up to the total daily limit of rounds).

This granted power is a supernatural ability.

Add Survival to your list of cleric class skills.

여행 계열 주문
  1. 1 긴걸음주자: Increases your speed.
  2. 2 물체 위치파악: Senses direction toward object (specific or type).
  3. 3 비행: Subject flies at speed of 60 ft.
  4. 4 차원의 문: Teleports you short distance.
  5. 5 순간이동: Instantly transports you as far as 100 miles/level.
  6. 6 길 찾기: Shows most direct way to a location.
  7. 7 순간이동, 상급: As teleport, but no range limit and no off-target arrival.
  8. 8 위상의 문: Creates an invisible passage through wood or stone.
  9. 9 영적 투사M: Projects you and companions onto Astral Plane.

속임수 계열

보장된 능력: Add Bluff, Disguise, and Hide to your list of cleric class skills.

속임수 계열 주문
  1. 1 변장: Disguise own appearance.
  2. 2 투명화: Subject invisible 1 min./level or until it attacks.
  3. 3 탐지방어M: Hides subject from divination, scrying.
  4. 4 혼란: Subjects behave oddly for 1 round/level.
  5. 5 거짓 광경M: Fools scrying with an illusion.
  6. 6 오도: Turns you invisible and creates illusory double.
  7. 7 가림막: Illusion hides area from vision, scrying.
  8. 8 객체 변이: Changes any subject into anything else.
  9. 9 시간 정지: You act freely for 1d4+1 rounds.

전쟁 계열

보장된 능력: Free Martial Weapon Proficiency with deity’s favored weapon (if necessary) and Weapon Focus with the deity’s favored weapon.

전쟁 계열 주문
  1. 1 마법 무기: Weapon gains +1 bonus.
  2. 2 영혼의 무기: Magical weapon attacks on its own.
  3. 3 마법의 예복: Armor or shield gains +1 enhancement per four levels.
  4. 4 신의 권능: You gain attack bonus, +6 to Str, and 1 hp/level.
  5. 5 화염 타격: Smite foes with divine fire (1d6/level damage).
  6. 6 칼날 방벽: Wall of blades deals 1d6/level damage.
  7. 7 권능의 말씀 눈멃: Blinds creature with 200 hp or less.
  8. 8 권능의 말씀 기절: Stuns creature with 150 hp or less.
  9. 9 권능의 말씀 죽음: Kills creature with 100 hp or less.

물 계열

보장된 능력: Turn or destroy fire creatures as a good cleric turns undead. Rebuke, command, or bolster water creatures as an evil cleric rebukes undead. Use these abilities a total number of times per day equal to 3 + your Charisma modifier. This granted power is a supernatural ability.

물 계열 주문
  1. 1 차폐의 안개: Fog surrounds you.
  2. 2 안개 구름: Fog obscures vision.
  3. 3 수중 호흡: Subjects can breathe underwater.
  4. 4 물 조종: Raises or lowers bodies of water.
  5. 5 얼음 폭풍: Hail deals 5d6 damage in cylinder 40 ft. across.
  6. 6 냉기 돌풍: 1d6/level cold damage.
  7. 7 산성 안개: Fog deals acid damage.
  8. 8 지독한 고사: Deals 1d6/level damage within 30 ft.
  9. 9 원소 무리*: Summons multiple elementals.

*Cast as a water spell only.
