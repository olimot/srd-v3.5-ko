                        기본 사항

                        THE BASICS

                        The Core Mechanic: Whenever you attempt an action that has some chance of failure, you roll a twenty-sided die (d20). To determine if your character succeeds at a task you do this:

                        • Roll a d20.

                        • Add any relevant modifiers.

                        • Compare the result to a target number.

                        If the result equals or exceeds the target number, your character succeeds. If the result is lower than the target number, you fail.

                        주사위

                        DICE

                        주사위 rolls are described with expressions such as “3d4+3,” which means “roll three four-sided 주사위 and add 3” (resulting in a number between 6 and 15). The first number tells you how many 주사위 to roll (adding the results together). The number immediately after the “d” tells you the type of die to use. Any number after that indicates a quantity that is added or subtracted from the result.

                        d%: Percentile 주사위 work a little differently. You generate a number between 1 and 100 by rolling two different ten-sided 주사위. One (designated before you roll) is the tens digit. The other is the ones digit. Two 0s represent 100.

                        분수의 반올림

                        ROUNDING FRACTIONS

                        In general, if you wind up with a fraction, round down, even if the fraction is one-half or larger.

                        Exception: Certain rolls, such as 피해 and 히트 포인트, have a minimum of 1.

                        곱하기

                        MULTIPLYING

                        Sometimes a rule makes you multiply a number or a die roll. As long as you’re applying a single multiplier, multiply the number normally. When two or more multipliers apply to any abstract value (such as a modifier or a die roll), however, combine them into a single multiple, with each extra multiple adding 1 less than its value to the first multiple. Thus, a double (×2) and a double (×2) applied to the same number results in a triple (×3, because 2 + 1 = 3).

                        When applying multipliers to real-world values (such as weight or distance), normal rules of math apply instead. A creature whose size doubles (thus 곱하기 its weight by 8) and then is turned to stone (which would multiply its weight by a factor of roughly 3) now weighs about 24 times normal, not 10 times normal. Similarly, a blinded creature attempting to negotiate difficult terrain would count each square as 4 squares (doubling the cost twice, for a total multiplier of ×4), rather than as 3 squares (adding 100% twice).

                        능력치와 주문시전자

                        ABILITIES AND SPELLCASTERS

                        The ability that governs bonus 주문 depends on what type of spellcaster your character is: 지능 for 마법사; 지혜 for 사제, 드루이드, 성기사, and 레인저; or 매력 for 소서러와 음유시인. In addition to having a high ability score, a spellcaster must be of high enough class 레벨 to be able to cast 주문 of a given spell 레벨. (See the class 묘사 for details.)

                        THE ABILITIES

                        Each ability partially describes your character and affects some of his or her actions.

                        근력 (Str)

                        STRENGTH (STR)

                        근력 measures your character’s muscle and physical power. This ability is especially important for 전사, 바바리안, 성기사, 레인저, and 수도사 because it helps them prevail in 전투. 근력 also limits the amount of 장비 your character can carry.

                        You apply your character’s 근력 modifier to:

                        • Melee 명중 굴림.

                        • 피해 rolls when using a melee weapon or a thrown weapon (including a sling). (Exceptions: Off-hand 공격 receive only one-half the character’s 근력 bonus, while two-handed 공격 receive one and a half times the 근력 bonus. A 근력 penalty, but not a bonus, applies to 공격 made with a bow that is not a composite bow.)

                        • 등반, 도약, and 수영 checks. These are the 스킬 that have 근력 as their key ability.

                        • 근력 checks (for breaking down 문 and the like).

                        민첩 (Dex)

                        DEXTERITY (DEX)

                        민첩 measures hand-eye coordination, agility, reflexes, and 균형. This ability is the most important one for 도적, but it’s also high on the list for characters who typically wear 빛 or medium 갑옷 (레인저 and 바바리안) or no 갑옷 at all (수도사, 마법사, and 소서러), and for anyone who wants to be a skilled archer.

                        You apply your character’s 민첩 modifier to:

                        • Ranged 명중 굴림, including those for 공격 made with bows, crossbows, throwing axes, and other ranged 무기.

                        • 방어도 (AC), provided that the character can react to the 공격.

                        • Reflex 내성 굴림, for avoiding 화염구 and other 공격 that you can escape by moving quickly.

                        • 균형, 탈출 전문가, 숨기, 조용히 이동, 자물쇠 열기, 탑승, 날렵한 손재주, 재주넘기, and 밧줄 사용 checks. These are the 스킬 that have 민첩 as their key ability.

                        건강 (Con)

                        CONSTITUTION (CON)

                        건강 represents your character’s health and stamina. A 건강 bonus increases a character’s 히트 포인트, so the ability is important for all classes.

                        You apply your character’s 건강 modifier to:

                        • Each roll of a Hit Die (though a penalty can never drop a result below 1—that is, a character always gains at least 1 hit point each time he or she advances in 레벨).

                        • Fortitude 내성 굴림, for resisting 독 and similar threats.

                        • 집중 checks. 집중 is a skill, important to spellcasters, that has 건강 as its key ability.

                        If a character’s 건강 score changes enough to alter his or her 건강 modifier, the character’s 히트 포인트 also increase or decrease accordingly.

                        지능 (Int)

                        INTELLIGENCE (INT)

                        지능 determines how well your character learns and reasons. This ability is important for 마법사 because it affects how many 주문 they can cast, how hard their 주문 are to resist, and how powerful their 주문 can be. It’s also important for any character who wants to have a wide assortment of 스킬.

                        You apply your character’s 지능 modifier to:

                        • The number of languages your character knows at the start of the game.

                        • The number of skill points gained each 레벨. (But your character always gets at least 1 skill point per 레벨.)

                        • 감정, 제작, 문자 해독, 장치 무력화, 위조, 지식, 탐색, and 주문학 checks. These are the 스킬 that have 지능 as their key ability.

                        A 마법사 gains bonus 주문 based on her 지능 score. The minimum 지능 score needed to cast a 마법사 spell is 10 + the spell’s 레벨.

                        An animal has an 지능 score of 1 or 2. A creature of humanlike 지능 has a score of at least 3.

                        지혜 (Wis)

                        WISDOM (WIS)

                        지혜 describes a character’s willpower, common sense, perception, and intuition. While 지능 represents one’s ability to analyze information, 지혜 represents being in tune with and aware of one’s surroundings. 지혜 is the most important ability for 사제 and 드루이드, and it is also important for 성기사 and 레인저. If you want your character to have acute senses, put a high score in 지혜. Every creature has a 지혜 score.

                        You apply your character’s 지혜 modifier to:

                        • Will 내성 굴림 (for negating the effect of 인간형 매혹 and other 주문).

                        • 치료, 듣기, 전문직, 의도 파악, 감지, and 생존 checks. These are the 스킬 that have 지혜 as their key ability.

                        사제, 드루이드, 성기사, and 레인저 get bonus 주문 based on their 지혜 scores. The minimum 지혜 score needed to cast a 사제, 드루이드, 성기사, or 레인저 spell is 10 + the spell’s 레벨.

                        매력 (Cha)

                        CHARISMA (CHA)

                        매력 measures a character’s force of personality, persuasiveness, personal magnetism, ability to lead, and physical attractiveness. This ability represents actual 근력 of personality, not merely how one is perceived by others in a social setting. 매력 is most important for 성기사, 소서러, and 음유시인. It is also important for 사제, since it affects their ability to turn undead. Every creature has a 매력 score.

                        You apply your character’s 매력 modifier to:

                        • 허세, 외교, 변장, 정보 수집, 동물 조련, 위협, 공연, and 마법 장치 사용 checks. These are the 스킬 that have 매력 as their key ability.

                        • Checks that represent attempts to influence others.

                        • Turning checks for 사제 and 성기사 attempting to turn 좀비, 뱀파이어, and other undead.

                        소서러와 음유시인 get bonus 주문 based on their 매력 scores. The minimum 매력 score needed to cast a 소서러 or 음유시인 spell is 10 + the spell’s 레벨.

                        When an ability score changes, all attributes associated with that score change accordingly. A character does not retroactively get additional skill points for previous 레벨 if she increases her 지능.
