                                                                                                전투 진행 방법

                                                                                                HOW COMBAT WORKS

                                                                                                전투 I cyclical; everybody acts in turn in a regular cycle of rounds. 전투 follows this sequence:

                                                                                                1. Each combatant starts out flat-footed. Once a combatant acts, he or she is no longer flat-footed.

                                                                                                2. Determine which characters are aware of their opponents at the start of the battle. If some but not all of the combatants are aware of their opponents, a 기습 round happens before regular rounds of 전투 begin. The combatants who are aware of the opponents can act in the 기습 round, so they roll for 선제권. In 선제권 order (highest to lowest), combatants who started the battle aware of their opponents each take one action (either a standard action or a 이동 action) during the 기습 round. Combatants who were unaware do not get to act in the 기습 round. If no one or everyone starts the battle aware, there is no 기습 round.

                                                                                                3. Combatants who have not yet rolled 선제권 do so. All combatants are now 준비 to begin their first regular round of 전투.

                                                                                                4. Combatants act in 선제권 order (highest to lowest).

                                                                                                5. When everyone has had a turn, the combatant with the highest 선제권 acts again, and steps 4 and 5 repeat until 전투 ends.
