                                            POWER LIST

                                            An Aappearing at the end of a power의 이름 in 파워 목록 denotes an augmentable power. An Xdenotes a power with an XP component paid by the manifester.

                                            Power Chains: Some powers reference other powers that they are based upon. Only information in a power later in the power chain that is different from the base power is covered in the power being described. Header entries and other information that are the same as the base power are not repeated. The same holds true for powers that are the equivalents of 주문, only the way the power varies from the spell is noted, such as power point cost.

                                            Order of Presentation: In 파워 목록 and 파워 설명 that follow them, the powers are presented in alphabetical order by 이름—except for those belonging to certain power chains and those that are psionic equivalents of 주문. When a power의 이름 begins with “하급,” “상급,” “광역,” or a similar kind of qualifier, the power 묘사 is alphabetized under the second word of the power 묘사 instead. When the effect of a power is essentially the same as that of a spell, the power의 이름 is simply “Psionic” followed by 이름 of the spell, and it is alphabetized according to the spell 이름.

                                            구현자 레벨: A power의 effect often depends on 구현자 레벨, which is the manifester의 psionic 직업 레벨. A creature with no classes has a 구현자 레벨 equal to its 히트 다이스 unless otherwise specified. The word “레벨” in the 파워 목록 always refers to 구현자 레벨.

                                            Creatures and Characters: “Creatures” and “characters” are used synonymously in 파워 설명.

                                            Augment: Many powers vary in 근력 depending on how many 파워 포인트 you put into them. The more 파워 포인트 you spend, the more powerful the manifestation. However, you can spend only a total number of points on a power equal to your 구현자 레벨, unless you have an ability that increases your effective 구현자 레벨.

                                            Many powers can be augmented in more than one way. When the Augment section contains numbered paragraphs, you need to spend 파워 포인트 separately for each of the numbered options. When a paragraph in the Augment section begins with “In addition,” you gain the indicated benefit according to how many 파워 포인트 you have already decided to spend on manifesting the power.
