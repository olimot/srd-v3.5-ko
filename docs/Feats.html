                                                                                                                                                                                                                                        전제조건

                                                                                                                                                                                                                                        PREREQUISITES

                                                                                                                                                                                                                                        Some 특기 have 전제조건. Your character must have the indicated ability score, class feature, feat, skill, 기본 공격 보너스, or other quality designated in order to select or use that feat. A character can gain a feat at the same 레벨 at which he or she gains the prerequisite.

                                                                                                                                                                                                                                        A character can’t use a feat if he or she has lost a prerequisite.

                                                                                                                                                                                                                                        특기 유형

                                                                                                                                                                                                                                        TYPES OF FEATS

                                                                                                                                                                                                                                        Some 특기 are general, meaning that no special rules govern them as a group. Others are 아이템 제작 특기, which allow spellcasters to create 마법 아이템 of all sorts. A 메타마법 feat lets a spellcaster prepare and 주문 시전 with 상급 effect, albeit as if the spell were a higher spell 레벨 than it actually is.

                                                                                                                                                                                                                                        전사 보너스 특기

                                                                                                                                                                                                                                        FIGHTER 보너스 FEATS

                                                                                                                                                                                                                                        Any feat designated as a 전사 feat can be selected as a 전사’s 보너스 feat. This designation does not restrict characters of other classes from selecting these 특기, assuming that they meet any 전제조건.

                                                                                                                                                                                                                                        아이템 제작 특기

                                                                                                                                                                                                                                        ITEM CREATION FEATS

                                                                                                                                                                                                                                        An item creation feat lets a spellcaster create a magic item of a certain type. Regardless of the type of items they involve, the various 아이템 제작 특기 all have certain features in common.

                                                                                                                                                                                                                                        XP Cost: Experience that the spellcaster would normally keep is expended when making a magic item. The XP cost equals 1/25 of the cost of the item in gold pieces. A character cannot spend so much XP on an item that he or she loses a 레벨. However, upon gaining enough XP to attain a new 레벨, he or she can immediately expend XP on creating an item rather than keeping the XP to advance a 레벨.

                                                                                                                                                                                                                                        Raw Materials Cost: The cost of creating a magic item equals one-half the sale cost of the item.

                                                                                                                                                                                                                                        Using an item creation feat also requires access to a laboratory or magical workshop, special tools, and so on. A character generally has access to what he or she needs unless unusual circumstances apply.

                                                                                                                                                                                                                                        Time: The time to create a magic item depends on the feat and the cost of the item. The minimum time is one day.

                                                                                                                                                                                                                                        Item Cost: 포션 제조, 마법막대 제작, and 마법두루마리 제작 create items that directly reproduce spell effects, and the power of these items depends on their 시전자 레벨—that is, a spell from such an item has the power it would have if cast by a spellcaster of that 레벨. The price of these items (and thus the XP cost and the cost of the raw materials) also depends on 시전자 레벨. 시전자 레벨 must be high enough that the spellcaster creating the item can cast the spell at that 레벨. To find the final price in each case, multiply 시전자 레벨 by the spell 레벨, then multiply the result by a constant, as shown below:

                                                                                                                                                                                                                                        두루마리: Base price = spell 레벨 x 시전자 레벨 x 25 gp.

                                                                                                                                                                                                                                        Potions: Base price = spell 레벨 x 시전자 레벨 x 50 gp.

                                                                                                                                                                                                                                        마법막대: Base price = spell 레벨 x 시전자 레벨 x 750 gp.

                                                                                                                                                                                                                                        A 0-레벨 spell is considered to have a spell 레벨 of 1/2 for 목적 of this calculation.

                                                                                                                                                                                                                                        Extra Costs: Any potion, scroll, or wand that stores a spell with a costly material component or an XP cost also carries a commensurate cost. For potions and 두루마리, the creator must expend the material component or pay the XP cost when creating the item.

                                                                                                                                                                                                                                        For a wand, the creator must expend fifty copies of the material component or pay fifty times the XP cost.

                                                                                                                                                                                                                                        Some 마법 아이템 similarly incur extra costs in material 구성요소 or XP, as noted in their 묘사.

                                                                                                                                                                                                                                        메타마법 특기

                                                                                                                                                                                                                                        METAMAGIC FEATS

                                                                                                                                                                                                                                        As a spellcaster’s 지식 of magic grows, she can learn to cast 주문 in ways slightly different from the ways in which 주문 were originally designed or learned. Preparing and casting a spell in such a way is harder than normal but, thanks to 메타마법 특기, at least it is possible.  주문 modified by a 메타마법 feat use a spell slot higher than normal. This does not change 레벨 of the spell, so the DC for 내성 굴림 against it does not go up.

                                                                                                                                                                                                                                        마법사 and 신성 Spellcasters: 마법사 and 신성 spellcasters must prepare their 주문 in advance. During preparation, the character chooses which 주문 to prepare with 메타마법 특기(and thus which ones take up higher-레벨 spell slots than normal).

                                                                                                                                                                                                                                        소서러와 음유시인: 소서러와 음유시인 choose 주문 as they cast them. They can choose when they cast their 주문 whether to apply their 메타마법 특기 to improve them. As with other spellcasters, the improved spell uses up a higher-레벨 spell slot. But because 소서러 or 음유시인 has not prepared the spell in a 메타마법 form in advance, he must apply 메타마법 feat on 감지. Therefore, such a character must also take more time to cast a 메타마법 spell (one enhanced by a 메타마법 feat) than he does to cast a regular spell. If the spell’s normal 시전 시간 is 1 action, casting a 메타마법 version is a full-round action for a 소서러 or 음유시인. (This isn’t the same as a 1-round 시전 시간.)

                                                                                                                                                                                                                                        For a spell with a longer 시전 시간, it takes an extra full-round action to cast the spell.

                                                                                                                                                                                                                                        Spontaneous Casting and 메타마법 특기: A 사제 spontaneously casting a cure or inflict spell can cast a 메타마법 version of it instead. Extra time is also required in this case. Casting a 1-action 메타마법 spell spontaneously is a full-round action, and a spell with a longer 시전 시간 takes an extra full-round action to cast.

                                                                                                                                                                                                                                        Effects of 메타마법 특기 on a Spell: In all ways, a 메타마법 spell operates at its original spell 레벨, even though it is prepared and cast as a higher-레벨 spell. 내성 굴림 modifications are not changed unless stated otherwise in the feat 묘사.

                                                                                                                                                                                                                                        The modifications made by these 특기 only apply to 주문 cast directly by the feat user. A spellcaster can’t use a 메타마법 feat to alter a spell being cast from a wand, scroll, or other device.

                                                                                                                                                                                                                                        메타마법 특기 that eliminate 구성요소 of a spell don’t eliminate 공격 of opportunity provoked by casting a spell while threatened. However, casting a spell modified by 빠른 주문 does not provoke an 공격 of opportunity.

                                                                                                                                                                                                                                        메타마법 특기 cannot be used with all 주문. See the specific 특기 설명 for 주문 that a particular feat can’t modify.

                                                                                                                                                                                                                                        Multiple 메타마법 특기 on a Spell: A spellcaster can apply multiple 메타마법 특기 to a single spell. Changes to its 레벨 are cumulative. You can’t apply the same 메타마법 feat more than once to a single spell.

                                                                                                                                                                                                                                        마법 아이템 and 메타마법 주문: With the right item creation feat, you can store a 메타마법 version of a spell in a scroll, potion, or wand. 레벨 limits for potions and 마법막대 apply to the spell’s higher spell 레벨(after the application of 메타마법 feat). A character doesn’t need 메타마법 feat to activate an item storing a 메타마법 version of a spell.

                                                                                                                                                                                                                                        Counterspelling 메타마법 주문: Whether or not a spell has been enhanced by a 메타마법 feat does not affect its vulnerability to counterspelling or its ability to counterspell another spell.
