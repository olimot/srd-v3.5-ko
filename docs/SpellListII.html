드루이드 주문

0 레벨 드루이드 주문 (소마법)

  • 물 생성: Creates 2 gallons/level of pure water.
  • 사소한 상처 치유: Cures 1 point of damage.
  • 마법 감지: Detects spells and magic items within 60 ft.
  • 독 감지: Detects poison in one creature or object.
  • 섬광: Dazzles one creature (–1 penalty on attack rolls).
  • 길라잡이: +1 on one attack roll, saving throw, or skill check.
  • 방향 알아내기: You discern north.
  • 빛: Object shines like a torch.
  • 수선: Makes minor repairs on an object.
  • 음식과 음료 정화: Purifies 1 cu. ft./level of food or water.
  • 마법 읽기: Read scrolls and spellbooks.
  • 저항: Subject gains +1 bonus on saving throws.
  • 미덕: Subject gains 1 temporary hp.

1 레벨 드루이드 주문

  • 평온한 동물: Calms (2d4 + level) HD of animals.
  • 동물 매혹: Makes one animal your friend.
  • 가벼운 상처 치유: Cures 1d8 damage +1/level (max +5).
  • 동식물 감지: Detects kinds of animals or plants.
  • 올무와 구덩이 감지: Reveals natural or primitive traps.
  • 정령 견디기: Exist comfortably in hot or cold environments.
  • 휘감기: Plants entangle everyone in 40-ft.-radius.
  • 페어리의 불꽃: Outlines subjects with light, canceling blur, concealment, and the like.
  • 굿베리열매: 2d4 berries each cure 1 hp (max 8 hp/24 hours).
  • 동물로부터 숨기: Animals can’t perceive one subject/level.
  • 도약: Subject gets bonus on Jump checks.
  • 긴걸음주자: Your speed increases by 10 ft.
  • 마법 송곳니: One natural weapon of subject creature gets +1 on attack and damage rolls.
  • 마법의 돌: Three stones gain +1 on attack rolls, deal 1d6+1 damage.
  • 차폐의 안개: Fog surrounds you.
  • 흔적 없는 이동: One subject/level leaves no tracks.
  • 화염 생성: 1d6 damage +1/level, touch or thrown.
  • 실레일리: Cudgel or quarterstaff becomes +1 weapon (1d10 damage) for 1 min./level.
  • 동물과 대화: You can communicate with animals.
  • 자연의 동료 I 소환: Calls creature to fight.

  • 에너지 저항: Ignores 10 (or more) points of damage/attack from specified energy type.

  • 거미 기어오르기: Grants ability to walk on walls and ceilings.
  • 자연의 동료 II 소환: Calls creature to fight.
  • 무리 소환: Summons swarm of bats, rats, or spiders.
  • 나무 형태: You look exactly like a tree for 1 hour/level.
  • 나무 왜곡: Bends wood (shaft, handle, door, plank).
  • 목재 조형: Rearranges wooden objects to suit you.
