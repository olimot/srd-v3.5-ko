COMBAT I (BASICS)

전투 진행 방법

HOW COMBAT WORKS

전투 I cyclical; everybody acts in turn in a regular cycle of rounds. 전투 follows this sequence:

1. Each combatant starts out flat-footed. Once a combatant acts, he or she is no longer flat-footed.

2. Determine which characters are aware of their opponents at the start of the battle. If some but not all of the combatants are aware of their opponents, a 기습 round happens before regular rounds of 전투 begin. The combatants who are aware of the opponents can act in 기습 round, so they roll for 선제권. In 선제권 order (highest to lowest), combatants who started the battle aware of their opponents each take one action (either a standard action or a 이동 action) during 기습 round. Combatants who were unaware do not get to act in the 기습 round. If no one or everyone starts the battle aware, there is no 기습 round.

3. Combatants who have not yet rolled 선제권 do so. All combatants are now 준비 to begin their first regular round of 전투.

4. Combatants act in 선제권 order (highest to lowest).

5. When everyone has had a turn, the combatant with the highest 선제권 acts again, and steps 4 and 5 repeat until 전투 ends.

선제권

INITIATIVE

선제권 Checks: At the start of a battle, each combatant makes an 선제권 check. An 선제권 check is a 민첩 check. Each character applies his or her 민첩 수정치 to the roll. Characters act in order, counting down from highest result to lowest. In every round that follows, the characters act in the same order (unless a character takes an action that results in his or her 선제권 changing; see 특수 선제권 행동).

If two or more combatants have the same 선제권 check result, the combatants who are tied act in order of total 선제권 수정치 (highest first). If there is still a tie, the tied characters should roll again to determine which one of them goes before the other.

Flat-Footed: At the start of a battle, before you have had a chance to act (specifically, before your first regular turn in 선제권 order), you are flat-footed. You can’t use your 민첩 보너스 to AC (if any) while flat-footed. 바바리안 and 도적 have the 기묘한 회피 extraordinary ability, which allows them to avoid losing their 민첩 보너스 to AC due to being flat-footed.

A flat-footed character can’t make 기회 공격.

When a 전투 starts, if you are not aware of your opponents and they are aware of you, you’re surprised.

Sometimes all the combatants on a side are aware of their opponents, sometimes none are, and sometimes only some of them are. Sometimes a few combatants on each side are aware and the other combatants on each side are unaware.

Determining awareness may call for 듣기 checks, 감지 checks, or other checks.

Any combatants aware of the opponents can act in 기습 round, so they roll for 선제권. In 선제권 order (highest to lowest), combatants who started the battle aware of their opponents each take a standard action during 기습 round. You can also take 자유 행동 during 기습 round. 전투 라운드

THE COMBAT ROUND

Each round represents 6 seconds in the game world. A round presents an opportunity for each character involved in a 전투 situation to take an action.

Each round의 activity begins with the character with the highest 선제권 result and then proceeds, in order, from there. Each round of a 전투 uses the same 선제권 order. When a character의 turn comes up in 선제권 sequence, that character 공연 his entire round의 worth of actions. (For exceptions, see 기회 공격 and 특수 선제권 행동.)

For almost all 목적, there is no relevance to the end of a round or the beginning of a round. A round can be a segment of game time starting with the first character to act and ending with the last, but it usually means a span of time from one round to the same 선제권 count in the next round. Effects that last a certain number of rounds end just before the same 선제권 count that they began on.
