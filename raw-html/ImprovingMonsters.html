템플릿

TEMPLATES

Certain creatures are created by adding a template to an existing creature. A templated creature can represent a freak of nature, the individual creation of a single experimenter, or the first generation of offspring from parents of different species.

템플릿 상속과 획득

ACQUIRED AND INHERITED TEMPLATES

Some 템플릿 can be added to creatures anytime. 템플릿 such as these are referred to as acquired 템플릿, indicating that the creature did not always have the attributes of the template.

Other 템플릿, known as inherited 템플릿, are part of a creature from the beginning of its existence. Creatures are born with these 템플릿.

It의 possible for a certain kind of template to be of either type.
