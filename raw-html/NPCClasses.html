직업 특성

All of the following are 직업 특성 of 주문 사용자 NPC class.

Weapon and Armor Proficiency: 주문 사용자 are skilled with all simple weapons. 주문 사용자 are not proficient with any type of armor nor with shields.

Spells: 주문 사용자 casts divine spells which are drawn from 주문 사용자 spell list (see below). Like a cleric, 주문 사용자 must choose and prepare her spells in advance. Unlike a cleric, 주문 사용자 cannot spontaneously cast cure or inflict spells.

To prepare or cast a spell, 주문 사용자 must have a Wisdom score equal to at least 10 + the spell level. The 난이도 등급 for a 내성 굴림 against 주문 사용자’s spell is 10 + the spell level + 주문 사용자’s Wisdom modifier.

주문 사용자, unlike wizards, do not acquire their spells from books or scrolls, nor do they prepare them through study. Instead, they meditate or pray for their spells, receiving them as divine inspiration or through their own strength of faith. Each 주문 사용자 must choose a time each day at which she must spend an hour in quiet contemplation or supplication to regain her daily allotment of spells. Time spent resting has no effect on whether 주문 사용자 can prepare spells.

Like other spellcasters, 주문 사용자 can cast only a certain number of spells of each spell level per day. Her base daily spell allotment is given on Table: 주문 사용자. In addition, she receives bonus spells per day if she has a high Wisdom score.

When Table: 주문 사용자 indicates that 주문 사용자 gets 0 spells per day of a given spell level, she gains only the bonus spells she would be entitled to based on her Wisdom score for that spell level.

Each 주문 사용자 has a particular holy symbol (as a divine focus) depending on 주문 사용자’s magical tradition.

Summon Familiar: At 2nd level, 주문 사용자 can call a familiar, just as a sorcerer or wizard can.

주문 사용자 주문 목록

주문 사용자 choose their spells from the following list.

0 Level: create water, cure minor wounds, detect magic, ghost sound, guidance, light, mending, purify food and drink, read magic, touch of fatigue.

1st Level: bless, burning hands, cause fear, command, comprehend languages, cure light wounds, detect chaos, detect evil, detect good, detect law, endure elements, obscuring mist, protection from chaos, protection from evil, protection from good, protection from law, sleep.

2nd Level: aid, animal trance, bear’s endurance, bull’s strength, cat’s grace, cure moderate wounds, darkness, delay poison, 투명상태, mirror image, resist energy, scorching ray, see 투명상태, web.

3rd Level: animate dead, bestow curse, contagion, continual flame, cure serious wounds, daylight, deeper darkness, lightning bolt, neutralize poison, remove curse, remove disease, tongues.

4th Level: cure critical wounds, minor creation, polymorph, restoration, stoneskin, wall of fire.

5th Level: baleful polymorph, break enchantment, commune, heal, major creation, raise dead, true seeing, wall of stone.
