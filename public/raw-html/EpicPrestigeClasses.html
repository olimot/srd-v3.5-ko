히트 다이스: d8.

EPIC BLACKGUARD

히트 다이스: d10.

레벨이 오를 때마다 추가 스킬 포인트: 2 + Int 수정치.

주문: 검은경비병’s 시전자 레벨 is equal to his or her class 레벨. 검은경비병’s number of 주문 per day does not increase after 10 레벨.

Smite Good: 에픽 건달 gains one additional daily use of smite good every 5 레벨 after 10th.  에픽 건달 adds his or her class 레벨 to 피해 with any smite good 공격, as normal.

언데드 명령: 검은경비병 명령 undead as a 사제 of two 레벨 lower, as normal.

Sneak 공격: 에픽 건달’s sneak 공격 피해 increases by +1d6 every three 레벨 after 10th.

Fiendish Servant: Up to 20th character 레벨, a fiendish servant’s powers depend on its master’s character 레벨, not his or her 검은경비병 class 레벨. After that, they depend on his or her 검은경비병 레벨. For every five 검은경비병 레벨 above 10th the fiendish servant gains +2 보너스 히트 다이스, its natural 갑옷 in-creases by +2, and its 근력 and 지능 each increase by +1.

타락한 성기사: A 검은경비병 who trades in more than ten 레벨 of 성기사 can gain more than ten 레벨 of 검은경비병, but only if his character 레벨 is 21st or higher.

보너스 특기: 에픽 건달 gains 보너스 feat (selected from the list of 에픽 건달 특기) every three 레벨 after 10th.
