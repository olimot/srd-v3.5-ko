상위직

상위직 offer a new form of multiclassing. Unlike the basic classes, characters must meet Requirements before they can take their first level of 상위직. The rules for level advancement apply to this system, meaning the first step of advancement is always choosing a class. If a character does not meet the Requirements for 상위직 before that first step, that character cannot take the first level of that 상위직.  Taking 상위직 does not incur the experience point penalties normally associated with multiclassing.

용어 정의

Here are definitions of some terms used in this section.

Base Class: One of the standard eleven classes.

Caster Level: Generally equal to the number of class levels (see below) in a spellcasting class. Some 상위직 add caster levels to an existing class.

Character Level: The total level of the character, which is the sum of all class levels held by that character.

Class Level: The level of a character in a particular class. For a character with levels in only one class, class level and character level are the same.
